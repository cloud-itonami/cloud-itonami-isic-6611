Administration of financial markets

ISIC Rev.5 6611

The problem: LLMs excel at intake normalization and threshold checks, but can fabricate jurisdiction requirements, overlook surveillance flags, or admit listings that don't meet your listing standard — all with no way to recover.

The solution: An independent Market Administration Governor holds every listing admission and trade-halt lift decision. It re-verifies against your jurisdiction facts, minimum market-cap threshold, and surveillance status before any action reaches production. No governor sign-off, no execution.

What this is not: This project does not claim to hold an exchange license, and does not perform full listing review (no public-float/shareholder-count analysis, no corporate-governance standards, no operating-history requirements). It provides the governed execution scaffold so you don't rebuild the compliance layer from scratch.
