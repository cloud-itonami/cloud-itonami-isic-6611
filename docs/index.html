Administration of financial markets

ISIC Rev.5 6611

An open-source business blueprint for exchange operators to fork and deploy. This actor governs listing intake, market-rule disclosure, surveillance screening, and listing admission with an independent Market Administration Governor and immutable audit ledger. No listing is admitted and no trade halt is lifted without human sign-off, ensuring compliance by construction.
